About Recipe Transformer

Recipe Transformer is a project for Professor Larry Birnbaum's EECS 337 course, Natural Language Processing at Northwestern University. The idea is to try and take existing recipies and transform them using a series of lists, trying to make the computer "read" and "understand" the recipes itself.

To limit the scope of the project, we are presented with original recipes from allrecipes.com.
